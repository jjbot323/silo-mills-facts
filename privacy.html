Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how silomillstruth.com ("the Site") collects, uses, and protects information that you provide when you visit the Site or sign our petition.

Information We Collect

When you sign the petition, we collect the following information that you voluntarily provide:

  • First and last name
  • Email address
  • ZIP code
  • Your role (e.g. resident, prospective homebuyer, community member)
  • Optional comment text

We do not automatically collect personally identifiable information from visitors who do not submit the petition form. We may collect anonymized technical information (browser type, referring page, approximate location) through standard web analytics tools.

How We Use Your Information

  • To count and verify petition signatures.
  • To provide updates about the Silo Mills environmental situation, if you opt in.
  • To respond to questions or comments you submit.
  • To aggregate anonymous statistics (e.g. total signature count, geographic distribution).

How We Share Your Information

We will not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We may share aggregated, anonymized data (for example, "over 500 residents have signed") publicly or with media outlets. We may disclose information if required by law or to enforce the Site's terms.

How We Protect Your Information

The Site is served over HTTPS with industry-standard SSL encryption. Form submissions are processed through our hosting provider's secure form backend and stored in access-controlled systems. We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards, but no Internet transmission is 100% secure.

Cookies and Analytics

The Site may use basic analytics tools to understand how visitors use the Site. These tools may use cookies or similar technologies. You can disable cookies through your browser settings at any time without affecting your ability to use the Site.

Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of the personal information you have submitted. Contact us through the information below to make such a request.

Children's Privacy

The Site is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent revision. Continued use of the Site after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact
